What is Reader's Paradise?

Reader's Paradise is a discord server founded by DIVINE_MDR on 21 August 2020.Our dream was and still is to reach out all the book lovers, readers and writers out there on discord and to create a helpful and enjoyable community for them, Let me mention some of the all things we do for our members.

We always try to keep our community friendly and helpful.
We host events for our members related to book stuff.
We also promote works of beginner writers.
Feel free to reach us out anytime, Our MODS will be there for you...

Why you should join us?

We provide a friendly and really helpful community for all book lovers out there, if you haven't started your bookish journey yet then no problem, Join us and we will show you the vast and interesting world of books. We are a welcoming community and we can provide you with almost every book you want and its absolutely free!!, Are you a writer or a server owner? If yes then don't worry!! We are here to promote your hardwork!!, We can assure you that you will have a great time in our community which is growing day by day.

FAQs

Is Reader's Paradise only book related?

Yes, Reader's Paradise is completely book related but you can also find people with different interests here.

Do you guys hold events?

Yes, whenever our MODS or ADMINS get time they host events. I will be honest here, Most of the time people don't participate.

Do you guys criticise members
over genre choices?

No, Absolutely No. We understand that every reader or any type of person have their own perspectives and choices about things and we respect them. This is a rule we follow in our server.

Do we pirate Ebooks?

Join us to know...."Smirks while typing".

Does a person need to be a reader
to join you?

It is not necessary that you can only join if you are a reader. As i mentioned before, We are a welcoming community, We welcome everyone with pleasure and happiness. Your choices, What you read/watch/write, Your gender, Nothing like that matters, We are here to serve you and it will be our pleasure to serve people and let them enjoy the vast world of books.
